My life's journey has been one of windy paths, rugged terrain and beautiful vistas. I've been here and there, done this and that. Backtracking due to deadends or deciding which direction to take are common realities in the pursuit of discovering something great.


Currently, I'm on a path toward becoming a UX designer. Maybe this endeavor will be worthwhile. Maybe not. Perhaps there's something even better out there. But, that's precisely the intrigue and beauty of life exploration: you can go this way and that way with the freedom to chart your own course.


In other words, it's the power of and.
