My life's journey has been one of windy paths, rugged terrain and majestic vistas.
I've been here and there, done this and that.
Backtracking due to deadends or
Choosing a direction to take
Are common realities
In the pursuit of
Discovering
Something
Great.


Currently, I'm on a path
Toward becoming a UX designer.
Maybe this endeavor will be worthwhile. Maybe not.
But, that's precisely the intrigue and beauty of life exploration:
You can go this way and that way with the freedom to chart your own course.


That's the power of and.
